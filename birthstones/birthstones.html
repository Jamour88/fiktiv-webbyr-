Om Din Sten

January - Garnet

The garnet symbolizes protection and commitment. It is often deep red in color, and represents strength and safety.

February - Amethyst

Amethyst, a purple stone, is believed to bring peace, calmness, and clarity of mind to its wearer.

March - Aquamarine

Aquamarine reflects the tranquility of water and is said to calm the soul, giving peace and clarity.

April - Diamond

The diamond symbolizes eternal love and strength, often associated with unbreakable bonds.

May - Emerald

Emerald, with its deep green hue, represents rebirth, love, and fertility. It is known to bring loyalty and wisdom.

June - Pearl

Pearls are timeless and represent purity and integrity, often symbolizing the moon’s energy.

July - Ruby

The ruby is a stone of passion, energy, and power, known for its vibrant red color that symbolizes love and vitality.

August - Peridot

Peridot, a green gem, is associated with strength, happiness, and harmony, bringing healing and balance.

September - Sapphire

Sapphire is a stone of wisdom and royalty, often representing loyalty, trust, and mental clarity.

October - Opal

Opal is believed to bring creativity and inspiration, with its unique play of colors symbolizing passion and spontaneity.

November - Topaz

Topaz brings warmth, strength, and intellect, often symbolizing friendship, creativity, and confidence.

December - Turquoise

Turquoise is a symbol of protection and healing, with its calming blue-green color representing truth and wisdom.
